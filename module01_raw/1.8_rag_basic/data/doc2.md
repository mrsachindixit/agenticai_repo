# Pune City

Pune, located in Maharashtra, India, is a major, rapidly growing metropolitan city known as the "Oxford of the East" for its prestigious educational institutions and as a premier IT and automobile hub. As the cultural capital of Maharashtra, it boasts a rich history as the former seat of the Maratha Empire. 

Key Aspects of Pune:
Economy: A prominent hub for IT, manufacturing, and engineering industries.
Education: Home to renowned institutions like Savitribai Phule Pune University, Symbiosis, and Fergusson College.
Culture & History: Rich in Maratha heritage, featuring landmarks like Shaniwar Wada and Lal Mahal. Known for the annual Savai Gandharva Music Festival and vibrant Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations.
Geography & Climate: Situated at the confluence of the Mula and Mutha rivers, it has a pleasant, tropical climate.
Living: Frequently ranked as one of the most livable cities in India, though facing challenges with rapid urbanization. 

With a population exceeding 7 million (metropolitan area), Pune is a blend of tradition and modern cosmopolitan life.
